Arrow keys,                    - Movement

Numpad keys

a                              - Invoke ability
l                              - Look mode
f                              - Fire mode
r                              - Reload
p                              - Pick up an item

Shift + ,                      - Look/Climb up
Shift + .                      - Look/Climb down

Shift + 2                      - Character screen
i                              - Inventory
m                              - View messages
?                              - Help
Shift + q                      - Quit to main menu
